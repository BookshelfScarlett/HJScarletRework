[b]空想归途（Lostbelt Journey）[/b]是旅人归途的附属模组，并受到游戏命运/冠位指定（Fate/Grand order）的影响，主要为旅人归途提供扩展性的游戏内容

[b]主要内容[/b]
[list]
[*]新职业代行者，来自异世的暴力机关。以武器样式的全面和命中叠层的处决攻击作为职业特色
[*]改造了原版的符文法师套装、鱼套装、牛仔套装、护花使者套装、和尚/渗透忍者套装、雨衣套装作为代行者的护甲，并提供了一套原创盔甲
[*]为旅人归途的所有长矛提供了经过深度打磨的投矛模式，并拥有一些原创投矛
[*]重置了旅人归途的部分飞刀特效，类似于VFX+
[*]新武器与新饰品
[/list]

[b]未来计划[/b]
[list]

[*]其余职业的武器扩展

[h1][b]The Root of Illusion has taken hold, and the destined worldline seems to have diverged...[/b][/h1]
[hr][/hr]
[b]Lostbelt Journey[/b] is an add-on mod for Homeward Journey, heavily inspired by Fate/Grand Order. It primarily provides expanded content for Homeward Journey

[b]Main Features[/b]
[list]
[*]New class: Executor, a violent enforcement agency from another world. Its class identity revolves around versatile weapon types and Execution Strikes triggered by stacking hits.
[*]Retools vanilla armor sets (Rune Wizard, Fish Costume, Cowboy, Floret Protector, Monk/Shinobi Ninja, Raincoat) as Executor armor, along with a set of original armor.
[*]Adds deeply polished throwing spear mode to all spears from Homeward Journey, plus some original throwing spears.
[*]Revamps VFX for certain throwing knives from Homeward Journey, similar to VFX+.
[*]New weapons and accessories.
[/list]

[b]Future Plans[/b]
[list]
[*]Expand the Executor's class depth and arsenal.
[*]Expand weaponry for other classes.
[*]Revamp visual effects of other weapons in Homeward Journey
[*]Type-Moon.
[/list]
