空想归途是一个（暂时）的旅人归途的附属模组，主要为旅人归途模组提供较大拓展性的游戏内容
并为旅人归途原有的内容做出优化
目前模组内为所有旅人长矛提供了经过深度打磨的投矛模式，并填充了一些原创投矛来保证完整的游戏流程
同时也有一些有待进行更多拓展的物品……
该模组仍然处于开发与打磨状态，包括特效、平衡等地方都需要进行一定程度的改进，感谢理解！


模组主要内容（目前）：
-为下方所有武器提供了投矛模式
 甜蜜一刺、野指针、滴蜡枪、黑暗之矛、蚀光矛、低吼、御手杆、归零针、进化、辩证法、电表镀针、逃逸速度
-为黑暗之矛与辩证法提供了重绘贴图（投矛版本限定）
-在流程内添加了一定数量的原创投矛保证投矛流程全覆盖
-四把制作质量超级高的锤子（也可能非常超模）！

未来计划：
-旅人归途的投刀类武器重置
-在不修改旅人归途武器攻击模组的情况下，优化武器的特效（类似于VFX+）
-数不清的原创内容扩展
-型月。

Lostbelt Journey is a (temporary) add-on mod for Homeward Journey, designed to provide expanded gameplay content and offer optimizations for the original features of Homeward Journey.
The mod currently introduces a polished javelin mechanics for all of Homeward Journey’s spears, along with several original javelins to support a complete gameplay experience.
It also includes some unique items that are still waiting to be further expanded.
This mod is still in active development and refinement. Effects, balance, and other aspects will continue to be improved. Thank you for your understanding!
Current Features:
-All of the weapons below now can be turned into Javelin:
 Sweet Sweet Stab, Wild Pointer, Candlance, Spear of Darkness, Light Bite, Deep Tone, Tonbogiri, Flyback Hand, Evolution, Dialectis, Galvanized Hand, Spear of Escape
-Provided a resprite for Spear of Darkness and Dialectis, javelin mode only
-Added some original javelin to fill up a full playthrough
-Four heavily polished thrown hammer.(might be overpowered too)

Future:
